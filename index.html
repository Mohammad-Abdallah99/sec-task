Salfit

Salfit is a Palestinian city located in the northern West Bank, known for its strategic position between several major cities. The area is rich in cultural heritage, featuring many archaeological sites and historical landmarks. Additionally, Salfit is famous for its olive cultivation, making it an important center for agricultural production in Palestine.
